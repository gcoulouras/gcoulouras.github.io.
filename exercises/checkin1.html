How to find articles from the Massachusetts Daily Collegian

The Massachusetts Daily Collegian is the official student-run newspaper of UMass Amherst. This page will list the two most popular ways to find the articles that we write

Navigate to...

Our website

Our different sections include:
  • News
  • Opinions
  • Arts & Living
  • Sports

Our Instagram

  1. Open Instagram and search @mdcollegian
  2. If the article was published in the last 24 hours, open our Instagram story to find a link to the article
  3. If the article was published over 24 hours ago, navigate to the Linktree in our bio that will take you to our website, where you can find the article
  4. Bonus: Open the 'Reels' tab on our Instagram page to see the video content the Collegian creates.
