About George

Introduction

Education

I am a junior at the University of Massachusetts Amherst pursuing a dual degree in English and Economics, as well as a certificate in Professional Writing and Technical Communication. I have also completed a minor in Psychology.

Writing Experience

I am also very involved with the Massachusetts Daily Collegian, the university's student-run newspaper, as a news writer and assistant news editor.

This semester, I will be starting a new position within The Center for Data Science and Artificial Intelligence as a Research Computing Technical Writing Intern.
